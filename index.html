My passion on web programming starts only after my graduation from HKU. Although my degree is not of computing related, I have great passion on web development which drives me to self-teach programming from basic. After my graduation, I start to teach myself basic html, css, javascript and ruby on rails. The main reason that I pick ruby on rails is that I also dream to have my own startup. After spending two to three month, I have developed a prototype of my own startup app.

My hobbies are reading, music and movies. Top of these should be running. Through running, I can stay healthy and refresh my mind for my daily tasks. It does help me a lot during coding. Hence, every time when I got stuck with some difficulties, I will put it away and go running for a while. The solutions will just then slip into your mind.

My short term dream is to become a professional full stack developer after my training on Accelerate HK. Having my own startup which contributes to society will be my long term dream. Besides, traveling around the world is one of my luxury dreams as well!
